# Sample Queries and Expected Behavior

## Query 1: Fair Use Factors
**Question:** What are the four factors considered in a fair use analysis?

**Expected behavior:** High confidence answer citing 17 U.S.C. § 107, listing:
1. Purpose and character of the use (commercial vs. nonprofit educational)
2. Nature of the copyrighted work
3. Amount and substantiality of the portion used
4. Effect on the potential market for the copyrighted work

## Query 2: Civil Rights Action
**Question:** Under what circumstances can a person sue a state official for civil rights violations?

**Expected behavior:** High confidence answer citing 42 U.S.C. § 1983, mentioning:
- Action under "color of" statute, ordinance, regulation, custom, or usage
- Deprivation of rights, privileges, or immunities secured by the Constitution
- Liability in an action at law, suit in equity, or other proper proceeding

## Query 3: Commercial Fair Use
**Question:** Does fair use apply to commercial uses of copyrighted material?

**Expected behavior:** Medium-to-high confidence answer explaining that commercial use is one factor considered but does not automatically preclude fair use.

## Query 4: Exclusive Rights
**Question:** What exclusive rights does a copyright owner have?

**Expected behavior:** High confidence answer citing § 106, listing reproduction, derivative works, distribution, public performance, public display, and digital audio transmission rights.

## Query 5: Out-of-Scope (Decline Expected)
**Question:** What is the penalty for insider trading under SEC regulations?

**Expected behavior:** System should respond with confidence "insufficient", explaining that the indexed corpus does not contain SEC or securities regulation documents.
